Build #1

Describe your product concept

A tool that parses through emails to gather and present financial transaction data. After parsing, it would store all the data on your device.

What are the different ways AI could solve this problem? What are the pros and cons of each? Which is the best option?

AI could track and store transactions. AI could send you personalized messages based on spending habits. AI could create predictions of spending to help manage a budget. The pros of parsing through emails is the availability of the Gmail API. Other ways, for example getting APIs from financial institutions, are much more difficult and costly.

How exactly would AI solve this problem?

AI could parse through emails using the Gmail API to collect transaction data. Then it could send useful information and insight directly to your phone.

What data or inputs would it need?

I would need to use the Google API to get access to emails.

What would a user's experience look like?

A user's experience would not be very involved. Most things would happen in the background. The user would receive updates in the form of short notifications or text messages.
